61/**
62 * @brief Describes a pair of internal Tensors after an operation.
63 *
64 * The most likely usage is that compute input comes from the imported_to
65 * TTL_int_tensor_t and the compute output goes to the to_export_from
66 * TTL_int_tensor_t.
67 *
68 */
